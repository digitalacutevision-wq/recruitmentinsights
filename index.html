Why It Matters

Recruitment Is the Foundation of Every Organisation

In India's hyper-competitive business environment, the difference between a company that scales and one that stagnates often comes down to one thing: the quality of people it hires. Effective recruitment is not just an HR function — it is a strategic business capability.

  • Speed matters: Companies that fill critical roles 30% faster than competitors gain measurable productivity advantages.
  • Cost of bad hiring: A wrong hire at a senior level can cost 2–3x the annual salary in lost productivity and rehiring expenses.
  • Talent is scarce: India has a growing demand-supply gap in leadership talent, particularly in IT, FMCG, and healthcare sectors.
  • Structured hiring wins: Organisations with formal recruitment processes report 40% higher quality of hire than those using ad-hoc methods.
